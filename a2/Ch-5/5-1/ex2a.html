Example 2a

Multiply Functions

Given:

$f(x) = x^2 + 7x + 12$

$g(x) = 3x - 4$

Find: $(f \cdot g)(x)$
$f(x) \cdot g(x)$

Select the correct substitution:

$(x^2 + 7x + 12)(3x - 4)$

Enter the coefficients for the distribution:

$x^2(3x) + x^2(-4) + 7x(3x) + 7x(-4) + 12(3x) + 12(-4)$
$3x^3$ $-$ $x^2$ $+$ $21x^2$ $-$ $x$ $+$ $36x$ $-$

$3x^3 - 4x^2 + 21x^2 - 28x + 36x - 48$

Combine like terms for the final result:

$3x^3 + $ $x^2 + $ $x - 48$

Complete!

$(f \cdot g)(x) = f(x) \cdot g(x)$
