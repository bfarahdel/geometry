Example 2a

Inverse Functions

$f(x) = 2x - 5$

Step 1

Change $f(x)$ to $y$.

$= 2x - 5$
Step 2
$y = 2x - 5$

Exchange $x$ and $y$ in the equation.

$x = 2$ $- 5$
Step 3
$x = 2y - 5$

Solve the equation for $y$.

$x + 5 = 2y$
$x + 5$
$= y$
Step 4
$y = \frac{x+5}{2}$

Replace $y$ with $f^{-1}(x)$.

$f^{-1}(x) = \frac{x+5}{2}$

The graph of $f^{-1}(x)$ is the reflection of the graph of $f(x)$ in the line $y=x$.
