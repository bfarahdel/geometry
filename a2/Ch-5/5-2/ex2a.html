Example 2a

Perform Composition of Functions

Given Functions:

$f = \{(1, 8), (0, 13), (15, 11), (14, 9)\}$

$g = \{(8, 15), (5, 1), (10, 14), (9, 0)\}$

Pair 1: $x=8$

Evaluate $g(8)$ first, then $f$:

$f[g(8)] = f(15)$ or
Show answer
Pair 2: $x=5$

Evaluate $g(5)$ first, then $f$:

$f[g(5)] = f(1)$ or
Show answer
Pair 3: $x=10$

Evaluate $g(10)$ first, then $f$:

$f[g(10)] = f(14)$ or
Show answer
Pair 4: $x=9$

Evaluate $g(9)$ first, then $f$:

$f[g(9)] = f(0)$ or
Show answer
