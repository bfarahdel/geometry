Example 1a: Find \(x\)
Step-by-Step Solution
  • 1. Identify Triangle Type: \(90^\circ - 45^\circ = 45^\circ\) This is a \(45^\circ\)-\(45^\circ\)-\(90^\circ\) triangle.
  • 2. Relationship: \(\text{Hypotenuse} = \text{Leg} \cdot \sqrt{2}\)
  • 3. Substitute and Solve: \(\text{Leg} = 6\) \(x = 6\sqrt{2}\)
Example 1b: Find \(x\)
Step-by-Step Solution
  • 1. Identify Triangle Type: Legs are congruent (\(\text{Leg} = 9\sqrt{2}\)). It is a \(45^\circ\)-\(45^\circ\)-\(90^\circ\) triangle.
  • 2. Relationship: \(\text{Hypotenuse} = \text{Leg} \cdot \sqrt{2}\)
  • 3. Substitute Value: \(x = (9\sqrt{2}) \cdot \sqrt{2}\)
  • 4. Simplify Answer: \(x = 9 \cdot (\sqrt{2} \cdot \sqrt{2})\) \(x = 9 \cdot 2 = 18\)
