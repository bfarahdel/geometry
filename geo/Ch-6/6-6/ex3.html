You are given the measure of the midsegment of a trapezoid and the measure of its bases. You are asked to find the measure of the top base.
Substitute the given expressions into the geometric equation.
\( 15 = \frac{1}{2}(x + 18.2) \)
What is the value of the midsegment?
Now that we have substituted the values, multiply each side by 2 to eliminate the fraction, then subtract 18.2 from each side to isolate \( x \).
Solving \( 30 = x + 18.2 \) leads to \( x = 11.8 \).
