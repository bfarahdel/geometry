A key property of a kite is that its diagonals are perpendicular. In kite \(WXYZ\), the diagonals \(\overline{WY}\) and \(\overline{XZ}\) intersect at point \(P\). Based on this, what is the measure of angle \(YPZ\)?
筝形的一个关键属性是其对角线互相垂直。在筝形 \(WXYZ\) 中，对角线 \(\overline{WY}\) 和 \(\overline{XZ}\) 相交于点 \(P\)。据此，角 \(YPZ\) 的度数是多少？
Since \(\angle YPZ\) is a right angle, \(\Delta YPZ\) is a right triangle. Use the Pythagorean Theorem to find the length of the hypotenuse, \(\overline{ZY}\).
\( PZ^2 + PY^2 = ZY^2 \)
Substitute the values (\(PZ = 8\), \(PY = 24\)) and enter the value for \(ZY^2\).
由于 \(\angle YPZ\) 是直角，因此 \(\Delta YPZ\) 是直角三角形。使用勾股定理求解斜边 \(\overline{ZY}\) 的长度。
\( PZ^2 + PY^2 = ZY^2 \)
代入值 (\(PZ = 8\), \(PY = 24\)) 并输入 \(ZY^2\) 的值。
We have \(640 = ZY^2\). To find \(ZY\), take the square root of both sides. Simplify \(\sqrt{640}\) into its simplest radical form, \(a\sqrt{b}\).
我们得出 \(640 = ZY^2\)。为了求出 \(ZY\)，对两边取平方根。将 \(\sqrt{640}\) 简化为最简根式形式 \(a\sqrt{b}\)。
