A kite is a quadrilateral with specific angle properties. Sum of all interior angles is always 360°. Referencing the diagram for Example 4a, what is the measure of angle G?
菱形（筝形）是具有特定角度属性的四边形。所有内角之和始终为 360°。参考 示例 4a 的图表，角 G 的度数是多少？
In a kite, exactly one pair of opposite angles is congruent. Since \(\angle G \neq \angle J\), then \(\angle F \cong \angle H\).
\( m\angle F + 128 + m\angle F + 72 = 360 \)
Simplify and solve for \(m\angle F\).
在菱形中，恰好有一对对角是全等的。由于 \(\angle G \neq \angle J\)，因此 \(\angle F \cong \angle H\)。
\( m\angle F + 128 + m\angle F + 72 = 360 \)
简化并求解 \(m\angle F\)。
By simplifying \(2m\angle F + 200 = 360\), we find that \(m\angle F = 80\).
通过简化 \(2m\angle F + 200 = 360\)，我们得出 \(m\angle F = 80\)。
